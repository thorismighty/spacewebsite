Why is The exploration of Space important?

Space is big. It is mind bogglingly big. It is so big that scientists are still struggling to really define exactly how big it is. Out there, in space, the possibilities are endless. The answers to our greatest questions are out there. Are we alone? Why are we here? Where is all of this(talking about our planet) headed? The only way we will ever find the answers to these questions, is by going there and looking. That is why manned exploration of space is so vitally important. All the probes and rovers and satellites in the world will never be the same as a human walking on another world and seeing it with their own eyes.
